My Career Goal

My goal is to help people who struggle with technology to have a better understanding of how it works and that it is there to help them, not hold them back.

My Motivation

I am motivated by the idea of giving people the patience that they deserve to learn at their own pace, and finding creative ways to teach people in any way that they might need, while also being able to help them with any issues that they may have in the workplace with tech, and helping them understand some of the troubleshooting process.
